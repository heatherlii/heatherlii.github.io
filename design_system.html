User Experience Design System

Overview

Describe project and my role.

During my internship at OpenText, I worked on their User Experience Design System which supports 100+ prodcuts across 12 product units, reporting directly to the Lead Designer. Using Figma and Web Site Management I created guidelines and designed specifications for various components and elements in the UX Design System to ensure consistency across multiple products.

Other contributions of mine include updating and refininf the component library by creating new interactive components, following the most recent specifications.
